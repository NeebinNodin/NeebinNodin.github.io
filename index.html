Greetings

Hello, my name is Neebinnodin Buswa and 34 years old. I'm half Ojibwe and Finnish and my name means 'Summer Wind'. I go by Neebin for the most part. I have been called by many variations of my name over the years, but I don't mind what names people come up with. As long as it's not offensive, I'm fine with it.

I currently live in Appleton. I lived most of my life in Michigan. I was born in Sault Ste Marie, Michigan, grow up in Two Rivers, Wisconsin, and spend most of my teens in Upper Peninsula of Michigan. I spent 14 years of my life in prison but I just recently got off of a two-year parole. Now I'm trying to further my education.

I'm currently in the IT Software Developer program. I'm in my second year of taking classes. When I first started at FVTC I was in the Industrail Engineering Technologies, but I switched my program August 2023.
